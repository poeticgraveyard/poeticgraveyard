Submit Your Work

Your words deserve to live somewhere. Send us your poetry, stories, confessions, and midnight thoughts.

How to Submit

Send your work to our editorial team. We accept poetry, short stories, letters never sent, confessions, essays, journals, and midnight thoughts. Anonymous submissions are welcome.

✉ poeticgraveyard@gmail.com
Open Email

Submission Guidelines

  • Submit in plain text format (no special formatting needed)
  • Include a title for your piece
  • Choose from: Poetry, Short Stories, Letters Never Sent, Confessions, Essays & Journals, Midnight Thoughts
  • You may use a pen name or submit anonymously
  • Tag your work for easier discovery (e.g., grief, love, memory)
  • Submissions are reviewed before being added to the archive

"Some words die unheard. We bury them here, beneath ink and starlight, where they bloom in the dark."

— The Poetic Graveyard Manifesto
