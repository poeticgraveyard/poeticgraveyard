What is Poetic Graveyard

A Sanctuary for the Written Word

Poetic Graveyard is a digital archive for poetry, short stories, letters never sent, confessions, essays, and midnight thoughts. It is a place where words that might otherwise be lost find a permanent home.

Each word deserves a beautiful place to rest in, this is a comfort hub for all the writers around the globe. For your thoughts deserve a place and your mind deserves equanimity.
